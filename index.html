Department of Computer Science & Engineering

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center for imparting quality technical education through innovative research and incubation with moral values in the field of computer science and engineering.

Department Overview

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was started in the year 2007 with a student intake of 60 to cater to the needs of software industries. Further, the intake was increased to 120 in the year 2009 and subsequently to 180, and 240 in the year 2014 and 2022 respectively. The Program Computer Science & Engineering is also accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for a period of 3 years up to 2026.

Our Mission

  • 📖 Fostering effective education through internal collaboration with industries to address societal demands.
  • 📖 Empowering professionals with strong ethical values and leadership abilities.
  • 📖 Developing research and entrepreneurship culture in students and faculty to cultivate lifelong learning.
